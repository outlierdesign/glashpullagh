Usage Rules

Do

One primary button per section. Use secondary for alternative actions. Ghost for nav links and back navigation.

Don't

Multiple primary buttons competing. Ghost buttons for important CTAs. Buttons without sufficient contrast on image backgrounds.

Cards

Four card variants for different content types across the site.

Standard Card

Timber Dams

Used in shallow drains with a thick peat layer. Untreated larch planks with a notch to direct water.

Stone Dams

Where the drain has eroded to the mineral layer. Slows flow and prevents widening.

Coir Logs

Biodegradable coconut husk rolls for blocking gullies and small drains.

Layout Patterns

Reusable layout patterns used across the site.

Split Content

Text left (50%), image right (50%). Used in: Restoration Tabs, About section. Stacks vertically on mobile.

Bento Grid

auto-fit, minmax(300px, 1fr). Cards with optional background images. Gold border on hover with lift.

Video Grid

auto-fit, minmax(320px, 1fr). Thumbnail with play icon overlay. Title and description below.

Hover Reveal Cards

Group hover: siblings blur + shrink. Active card: scale up + clear. Used for Slowing The Flow section.

Carousel

Embla carousel with dot pagination. 3 cards visible on desktop, 1 on mobile. Category badge top-left.

Parallax Break

60-70vh height. Fixed background-attachment or photo + gradient overlay. Centered text content.

Interaction Standards

Hover Lift

translateY(-4px) + border-color to --gold-light. Duration: 0.4s ease-out-expo.

Hover Scale

scale(1.02) for gallery items. scale(1.04) for blog card backgrounds. Duration: 0.5s.

Fade In

Opacity 0→1 with translateY(10px→0). Gallery titles, tooltips. Duration: 0.4s.
